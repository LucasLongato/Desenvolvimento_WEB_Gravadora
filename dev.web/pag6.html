    David Lawrence Brewster Jr. (born June 3, 1988), better known by his stage name Dave East, is an American rapper from Harlem, New York. East began his career in 2010, and gained attention in 2014 with his eighth mixtape, Black Rose. Its release caught the attention of New York rapper Nas, who later signed East to his label, Mass Appeal Records, and later in conjunction with Def Jam Recordings.
