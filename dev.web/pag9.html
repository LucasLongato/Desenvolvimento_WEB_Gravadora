    Denzel Rae Don Curry (born February 16, 1995) is an American rapper and artist. Born and raised in Carol City, Florida, Curry began rapping in sixth grade and began working on his first mixtape in 2011. Influenced by Florida underground rapper SpaceGhostPurrp, the mixtape was later featured on Purrp's social media, garnering attention. Curry into the local music scene and resulting in him joining Purrp's hip-hop collective Raider Klan.
