    Armando Christian Perez (January 15, 1981), better known by his stage name Pitbull, is an American DJ, businessman, rapper, singer and songwriter of Cuban descent. Pitbull, began to gain notoriety after participating in a song by Lil Jon, which helped the rapper start his career, in addition to participating in the soundtrack of the movie 2 Fast 2 Furious. In 2004, Pitbull released his first album, called "M.I.A.M.I.", which went gold in the United States, then he released the albums "El Mariel" and "The Boatlift" in 2007 and 2008 respectively. In 2009, he released "Rebelution" which appeared on several charts, as well as releasing "I Know You Want Me (Calle Ocho)", the first worldwide hit single.
