    The LOX (or The Lox) is an American hip hop musical group formed in 1994 in Yonkers, New York. The group consists of rappers Sheek Louch, Styles P and Jadakiss. They were originally signed to Sean Combs' Bad Boy Records label in 1995, before joining Ruff Ryders Entertainment in 1999. They have since launched their own label, called "D-Block Records". The D-Block name represents the collective label of the group.
